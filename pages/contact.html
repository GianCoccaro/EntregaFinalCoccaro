ARGENTINA

We are currently in the beautiful country of Argentina, located at the Obelisco in the heart of the capital city, Buenos Aires. During our time here, we've fallen in love with its vibrant culinary culture. The famous Argentine asado, mouthwatering empanadas, and the delightful fusion of flavors in Buenos Aires' cuisine have captivated our taste buds. Sharing mate with locals has also been a heartwarming experience. Argentina's diverse regional dishes have left an unforgettable mark on us, and we'll cherish these culinary memories for a lifetime.
To get in touch with us, feel free to reach out using the logos in the bottom bar or by visiting our location from Monday to Friday, between 8 am and 3 pm.
